Role Waver Remote / 3D Head Scan / Digital Twin

OBJECTIVE —

The vision can only be achieved by pure passion and creativity

( ビジョンは純粋な情熱によってのみ達成できます と創造性)

The visionary of IC4D and Waver Remote (Rome) would like to reach out for help in achieving a dream. The dream is that people may express themselves in ways they never had access to before. There is a need to inspire creativity, to help others build their own worlds and realities that live past those on a screen. Imagine having the ability to create everything in front of you and be inspired by the creations of others.

02 —

When you believe a thing, believe it all the way, implicitly and unquestionably.

Good designers must always be avant-gardists, always one step ahead of the times. They should–and must–question everything generally thought to be obvious. They must have an intuition for people’s changing attitudes. For the reality in which they live, for their dreams, their desires, their worries, their needs, their living habits. They must also be able to assess realistically the opportunities and bounds of technology.

03 —

Design is a solution to a problem. Art is a question to a problem.

The future belongs to a different kind of person with a different kind of mind: artists, inventors, storytellers-creative and holistic ‘right-brain’ thinkers whose abilities mark the fault line between who gets ahead and who doesn’t.
