Account Manager

Known for being a top inbound and content marketing agency, Boro Startup is constantly innovating, experimenting and offering our customers access to new marketing and sales services, technologies and ideas. Our team of all-star marketing experts were top performers in the field before coming to Kuno. Now we harness our collective creativity and digital marketing capabilities to help our clients achieve their business goals.

As an account manager at Boro, you will ensure clients receive requested products and services in a timely fashion, communicate client needs and demands, and forecast and track client account metrics. Ultimately, you will manage projects within client relationships, working to carry out client goals while meeting company goals.

Requirements:
  • Coordinate client services for key accounts while working closely with the Executive Team as well as service experts
  • Be a point of contact for client communications
  • Identify challenges and solutions, report on key performance indicators (KPIs), and garner client  feedback
  • Coordinate with staff members working on the same account to ensure consistent service
  • Service multiple clients concurrently, often meeting deadlines
Education & Experience
  • Bachelor’s Degree in marketing or relevant field
  • 3-5 years experience in digital marketing
  • Familiarity with digital platforms and social media
  • Valid Inbound Marketing and HubSpot Certifications
  • Not Required, but a plus if candidate is SEMrush Certified, Google AdWords Search/Display Certified, and Facebook Marketing Certified
  • Experience with Google Ads, Bing Ads, and Facebook Ads
  • Experience with SEO software, which includes SEM Rush, MOZ or AHREFs
